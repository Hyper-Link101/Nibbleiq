## ‚úÖ Immediate Actions Required

### 1. Submit to Google Search Console (MOST IMPORTANT)
**This is the #1 priority to get indexed quickly**

#### Steps:
1. Go to [Google Search Console](https://search.google.com/search-console/)
2. Click "Add Property"
3. Enter: `https://siftiq.io`
4. Verify ownership using one of these methods:
   - **HTML file upload** (easiest for developers)
   - **HTML meta tag** (add to your site header)
   - **Google Analytics** (if already installed)
   - **DNS record** (if you control DNS)

5. Once verified, click "Sitemaps" in the left menu
6. Submit your sitemap: `https://siftiq.io/sitemap.xml`
7. Click "URL Inspection" at the top
8. Enter: `https://siftiq.io`
9. Click "Request Indexing"

**Expected Timeline:** Google will crawl within 24-48 hours

### 2. Submit to Bing Webmaster Tools

4. Verify ownership
5. Submit sitemap: `https://siftiq.io/sitemap.xml`
